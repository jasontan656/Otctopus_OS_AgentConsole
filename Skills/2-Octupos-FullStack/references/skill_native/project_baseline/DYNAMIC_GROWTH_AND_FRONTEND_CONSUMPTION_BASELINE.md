# DYNAMIC_GROWTH_AND_FRONTEND_CONSUMPTION_BASELINE

- The authored-doc tree is intentionally dynamic and may grow new branches when the project grows.
- The skill owns the growth rules; authored-doc content grows by current project needs rather than a frozen static sitemap.
- `Admin_UI` and future front-end consumers must discover authored-doc scopes dynamically and compose them for humans at runtime.
- Offline storage stays fragmented and machine-first; online presentation may aggregate, reorder, and visually connect the same content for human reading.
- Growth must stay rule-driven: new branches need navigation exposure, status blocks, and future OS_graph compatibility.
